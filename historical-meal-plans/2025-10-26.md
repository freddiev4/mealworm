# Sunday

Lunch:
Leftover Seared Tuna Salad (from Sunday dinner)

Dinner: Seared Sesame-Ginger Tuna Steaks with Sautéed Bok Choy & Jasmine Rice ([recipe](https://www.snixykitchen.com/seared-sesame-crusted-tuna-with-orange-ginger-soy-sauce/))

### Ingredients:
- 2 tuna steaks (ahi or yellowfin)
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds (black/white mix optional)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 heads baby bok choy (or 1 large), halved
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 cup jasmine rice (dry)
- Lime wedges

Sauce: ginger-soy glaze (soy + ginger + sesame oil) drizzled over tuna and bok choy.

# Monday

## Lunch:
Leftover Chicken Mole bowls (from Monday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Shredded leftover mole chicken
- Mexican rice (leftover)
- Roasted zucchini (leftover)
- Cilantro, lime wedges

## Dinner:
Slow-Cooker Chicken Mole with Mexican Rice & Roasted Zucchini ([recipe](https://www.allrecipes.com/slow-cooker-chicken-mole-recipe-11821021))

### Ingredients:
- 1.5–2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs or breasts
- 2–3 tbsp mole paste or ingredients to make mole (ancho chiles, chocolate, almonds/peanuts, spices) — see recipe link
- 1 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 cup rice (for Mexican rice)
- 2 medium zucchini, halved and roasted
- Vegetable oil

Sauce: rich mole sauce (chocolate-chile sauce) — serves as the main sauce on the chicken and rice.

# Tuesday

## Lunch:
Leftover Coconut Shrimp Curry with Rice (from Tuesday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover shrimp curry
- Steamed rice
- Fresh cilantro and lime wedges

## Dinner:
Thai Coconut Shrimp Curry with Vegetables & Rice ([recipe](https://www.garnishandglaze.com/thai-coconut-curry-shrimp-recipe/))

### Ingredients:
- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled & deveined
- 1–2 tbsp red or yellow curry paste
- 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas or green beans
- 1 small onion
- 1 tbsp fish sauce (or soy)
- 1 tbsp brown sugar or palm sugar
- 1 cup jasmine rice (dry)
- Fresh basil or cilantro for garnish

Sauce: creamy coconut-curry sauce — plentiful for leftover lunches.

# Wednesday

## Lunch:
Leftover Baked Ziti with Sausage (from Wednesday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover baked ziti portion
- Side salad greens

## Dinner:
Baked Ziti with Italian Sausage & Marinara ([recipe](https://www.onceuponachef.com/recipes/baked-ziti-with-sausage.html))

### Ingredients:
- 12 oz ziti or penne pasta
- 1 lb Italian sausage (bulk or removed from casings)
- 3 cups marinara sauce (jarred or homemade)
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 1½–2 cups shredded mozzarella
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- 1 head broccoli or a big bunch of kale, roasted or sautéed as a vegetable side

Sauce: marinara mixed into ziti and baked; extra sauce for leftovers.

# Thursday

## Lunch:
Leftover Chipotle-Lime Pork & Roasted Veg bowl (from Thursday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover sliced pork tenderloin
- Roasted carrots & any other roasted veg
- Cilantro-lime crema drizzle

## Dinner:
Chipotle-Lime Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Carrots & Avocado Crema ([recipe](https://www.wrightfamilytable.com/chipotle-lime-grilled-pork-tenderloin/))

### Ingredients:
- 1.5–2 lb pork tenderloin
- 2 limes (zest + juice)
- 1–2 tsp chipotle powder or 1 tbsp adobo (adjust heat)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lb carrots, peeled & roasted
- 1 avocado
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream (for crema)
- Cilantro, garlic

Sauce: avocado crema (avocado + yogurt + lime + cilantro) and a chipotle-lime pan sauce for pork.

## Dinner:
Baked Eggplant Parmesan with Marinara & Roasted Green Beans ([recipe](https://www.onceuponachef.com/recipes/eggplant-parmesan.html))

### Ingredients:
- 1–2 medium eggplants
- 2 cups marinara sauce (jarred or homemade)
- 1–1½ cups shredded mozzarella
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- Breadcrumbs or panko (if breading)
- 1 lb green beans, trimmed & roasted
- Olive oil, garlic, basil

Sauce: marinara over layered eggplant and cheese; excellent as leftovers for Monday lunch the following week.
