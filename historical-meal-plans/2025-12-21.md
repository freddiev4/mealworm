# Monday

## Lunch: 
Salad with chickpeas

### Ingredients:
- Chickpeas
- Salad greens
- Vinaigrette or extra marinara

## Dinner: Sheet-Pan Lemon-Garlic Cod with Roasted Vegetables ([recipe](https://www.eatingwell.com/recipe/267619/sheet-pan-lemon-garlic-cod-with-roasted-vegetables/))

### Ingredients:
- Cod fillets (4 fillets)
- Zucchini, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, red onion (or seasonal roasting veg)
- Baby potatoes (optional)
- 2 lemons (zest + juice)
- 3 tbsp butter or olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Fresh parsley or dill
- Salt & pepper

Sauce: lemon-garlic herb butter (drizzle over cod + veggies) — ensures the dish is saucy and not dry.

## Lunch:
Lunch at mom's house

## Dinner:
Mushrooms Carnitas Burrito Bowls with Cilantro-Lime Rice & Avocado Crema ([recipe/inspo](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/jeff-mauro/slow-cooker-carnitas-with-avocado-crema-5271289))

Sauce: avocado crema drizzled on bowls; carnitas also get a finishing salsa or crema.

# Friday

## Lunch:
Leftover mushroom carnitas, with tortillas

## Dinner:
Marry Me Chicken (creamy sun-dried tomato & Parmesan sauce) with rice & green beans ([recipe](https://www.caileeeats.com/recipes/marry-me-chicken))

### Ingredients:
- 1.5–2 lb chicken thighs or breasts
- Salt & pepper, garlic powder
- 1 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup grated Parmesan
- 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes (in oil), optional
- Red pepper flakes, thyme/oregano
- Cooked rice or pasta
- Steamed green beans or roasted asparagus

Sauce: creamy Parmesan + sun-dried tomato sauce — rich and saucy so leftovers stay moist. (This is the single chicken dish for the week.)
