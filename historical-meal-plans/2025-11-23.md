Lunch:

- Cherry tomatoes

Dinner: Chile-Lime Mahi-Mahi Tacos with Chipotle Crema
Recipe: https://www.joyfulhealthyeats.com/grilled-mahi-mahi-fish-tacos-with-chipotle-lime-crema/

### Ingredients:
- 1 lb mahi-mahi fillets (or firm white fish)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1-2 limes (zest + juice)
- 1 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp cumin, salt & pepper
- Corn tortillas
- Purple cabbage (thinly sliced)
- Carrot (julienned)
- Cilantro, lime wedges
- For chipotle crema: mayo (or Greek yogurt) + adobo chipotle + lime + a little honey

Notes: Leftovers make a great taco/bowl for Monday lunch (fish reheats or served cold).

## Dinner: Chicken Tinga Tacos (only chicken dish this week)
Recipe: https://www.gimmesomeoven.com/chicken-tinga-tacos/

### Ingredients:
- 1.5–2 lb cooked shredded chicken (breasts or thighs)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2–3 cloves garlic
- 1–2 chipotle peppers in adobo + 1/2 cup canned tomatoes (or fire-roasted tomatoes)
- 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp oregano, salt & pepper
- Corn tortillas or lettuce cups
- Toppings: sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, diced onion, lime

Notes: Saucy shredded chicken keeps well for Tuesday lunch.

## Dinner: Miso-Glazed Cod with Baby Bok Choy
Recipe: https://www.cleaneatingmag.com/recipes/miso-glazed-cod-spicy-orange-baby-bok-choy-recipe/

### Ingredients:
- 4 cod fillets (or black cod / sablefish if available)
- 2–3 tbsp white miso paste
- 1–2 tbsp mirin (or rice wine) + 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tbsp brown sugar or honey
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Baby bok choy (halved)
- Garlic, ginger
- Cooked rice or soba
- Sesame seeds, green onions for garnish

Sauce: sweet-savory miso glaze that coats the fish and veggies. Leftovers are great for Wednesday lunch.
