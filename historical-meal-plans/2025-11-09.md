# Sunday

Lunch: Leftover Sheet-Pan Lemon-Garlic Cod with Roasted Vegetables (from Sunday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover cod pieces
- Leftover roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes)
- Lemon wedges
- Fresh parsley

Dinner: Sheet-Pan Lemon-Garlic Cod with Roasted Vegetables ([recipe](https://www.eatingwell.com/recipe/7925589/sheet-pan-lemon-garlic-cod-with-roasted-vegetables/))

### Ingredients:
- Cod fillets (4)
- Zucchini, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes (mixed roasting veg)
- Lemon (zest + juice)
- Garlic
- Olive oil or butter
- Fresh parsley or dill
- Salt & pepper

Sauce: Lemon-garlic herb butter/drizzle (recipe link above). Vegetables roast on the same sheet pan so every bite includes veg. Leftovers pack and reheat well for Monday lunch.

# Monday

## Lunch:
Leftover Beef Bolognese over rigatoni (from Monday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover bolognese sauce & rigatoni
- Shaved Parmesan
- Roasted broccoli (leftover)

## Dinner:
Rigatoni Bolognese (classic beef ragu) with Roasted Broccoli ([recipe](https://thealmondeater.com/the-easiest-rigatoni-bolognese/))

### Ingredients:
- Ground beef (1–1.5 lb)
- Onion, carrot, celery (sofrito)
- Garlic
- Canned crushed tomatoes
- Tomato paste
- Red wine or beef stock
- Rigatoni pasta
- Parmesan cheese
- Broccoli (for roasting)
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

Sauce: Rich tomato-meat ragu (recipe link). Serve with roasted broccoli tossed in olive oil and lemon — makes a saucy dinner and leftover-friendly lunch.


# Tuesday

## Lunch:
Leftover Rigatoni Bolognese (from Monday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover rigatoni + bolognese
- Parmesan
- Steamed or roasted green veg (broccoli or green beans)

## Dinner:
Grilled fish tacos with Chipotle Crema & Cabbage Slaw ([recipe](https://www.aquastar.com/recipes/mahi-mahi-fish-tacos-with-chipotle-crema/))

### Ingredients:
- fish fillets (or other firm white fish)
- Corn or flour tortillas
- Shredded cabbage or slaw mix
- Lime
- Cilantro
- Avocado (optional)
- For chipotle crema: mayo or sour cream, lime, chipotle in adobo (or chipotle powder), garlic
- Olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt & pepper

Sauce: Chipotle crema drizzled over tacos. Includes cabbage slaw (veg) on every taco. Leftovers: fish & slaw make excellent taco bowls for Wednesday lunch.


# Wednesday

## Lunch:
Leftover Mahi-Mahi taco filling served as a bowl with rice/greens (from Tuesday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover mahi-mahi pieces
- Leftover slaw
- Rice or mixed greens
- Chipotle crema

## Dinner:
Chicken Marsala with Mushrooms & Roasted Asparagus ([recipe](https://www.onceuponachef.com/recipes/chicken-marsala.html))

### Ingredients:
- Chicken breasts or cutlets (this is the only chicken dish this week)
- Mushrooms (cremini or button)
- Marsala wine
- Chicken stock
- Butter & olive oil
- Shallot or onion, garlic
- Fresh parsley
- Asparagus (roasted)
- Flour for dredging (optional), salt & pepper

Sauce: Marsala wine + mushroom pan sauce — rich and saucy so the chicken is never dry. Leftovers store/reheat well for Thursday lunch.


# Thursday

## Lunch:
Leftover Chicken Marsala over rice or noodles (from Wednesday dinner)

### Ingredients:
- Leftover chicken + mushroom-Marsala sauce
- Rice, mashed potatoes, or pasta
- Roasted asparagus (leftover)

## Dinner:
Super-easy: Oven-Baked Frozen Turkey Burgers + Oven Fries (mostly pre-made/frozen)

Recipe / how-to: How to cook frozen burgers in the oven (guidelines) ([guide](https://getonmyplate.com/perfect-frozen-burgers-in-the-oven/))

### Ingredients:
- Frozen turkey burger patties (store-bought)
- Burger buns
- Sliced cheese (optional)
- Lettuce, tomato, red onion
- Frozen oven fries or sweet potato fries
- Ketchup, mayo, mustard, or spicy mayo (sauce)
- Pickles optional (you dislike olives/capers so skip those)

Sauce: burger sauce or mayo/ketchup/spicy mayo — every bite saucy. Serve with a side salad or roasted veggies to ensure veg with the meal. Leftovers: burger patties and fries refrigerate and are easy to reheat for Friday lunch.
