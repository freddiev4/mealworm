## Dinner: Blackened Cod with Mango Salsa + Roasted Sweet Potatoes & Green Beans ([recipe](https://www.glitterandgraze.com/blackened-mahi-mahi-with-mango-salsa/))

### Ingredients:

- Cod fillets (4 fillets, ~1–1.25 lb)
- Blackening spice mix (paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, salt)
- Olive oil or butter for searing
- Mango (1 large or 2 small), diced
- Red bell pepper, diced
- Red onion or shallot, finely diced
- Jalapeño (optional), cilantro, lime juice — for mango salsa
- Sweet potatoes (2–3), cut into wedges
- Green beans or broccolini, tossed in olive oil, salt & pepper

Sauce: fresh mango salsa spooned over fish. Dinners are portioned so leftovers are great for Monday lunch.

# Tuesday

## Lunch: Leftover Blackened Cod Bowl (from Sunday dinner)

### Ingredients:

- Leftover cod flaked
- Leftover roasted sweet potatoes
- Leftover green beans or a quick salad

## Dinner: Rigatoni alla Vodka (Creamy Tomato Rigatoni) + Roasted Brussels Sprouts ([recipe](https://www.seriouseats.com/pasta-with-vodka-sauce))

### Ingredients:

- Rigatoni pasta (12–16 oz)
- Crushed tomatoes or tomato puree (1 can)
- sour cream (1/2–3/4 cup)
- Onion, garlic
- Red pepper flakes, salt & pepper
- Olive oil, butter
- Parmesan cheese to finish
- Brussels sprouts, halved and roasted with olive oil

Sauce: vodka-style tomato cream sauce (rich and saucy). Leftovers reheat well for Tuesday lunch.

# Thursday

## Lunch: Leftover Rigatoni alla Vodka

## Dinner: Baked Ziti with Ricotta & Roasted Asparagus ([recipe](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/18031/baked-ziti-ii/))

### Ingredients:

- Ziti or penne (12–16 oz)
- Ricotta cheese (1–1.5 cups)
- Marinara sauce (3 cups) — jarred or homemade
- Mozzarella & Parmesan cheeses
- Garlic, onion, olive oil
- Asparagus, trimmed and roasted with olive oil

Sauce: marinara mixed into the pasta and ricotta — very saucy. Leftovers are ideal for Thursday lunch.

# Friday

## Lunch: Leftover Baked Ziti

### Ingredients:

- Leftover ziti warmed with extra sauce if needed
- Side salad or extra roasted veg

## Dinner: Carne Asada Bowls with Cilantro-Lime Rice & Grilled Veggies ([recipe](https://whitneybond.com/carne-asada-bowl/))

### Ingredients:

- Skirt or flank steak (1.5–2 lb) marinated in lime, garlic, olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt
- Cilantro-lime rice (white rice + lime juice + chopped cilantro)
- Grilled bell peppers and onions (or charred corn)
- Black beans (optional)
- Lime wedges, sliced avocado

Sauce: top bowls with a tangy salsa verde or extra chimichurri (keeps the bowl saucy). Leftovers work great for Friday lunch.

## Dinner: Super-Easy Frozen Chicken Burgers + Oven Fries + Quick Slaw (mostly pre-made/frozen option)

### Ingredients:

- Frozen chicken burger patties (brand of choice) — cook per package
- Burger buns
- Frozen oven fries or sweet potato fries
- Lettuce, tomato, sliced onion, pickles
- Quick slaw: bagged coleslaw mix + rice vinegar + mayo (or store-bought slaw dressing)

Sauce: chili-crisp mayo (mayonnaise + chili crisp) or smoky aioli — spoon over the burger. This is the only chicken dish this week. Leftovers are easy for Saturday lunch.

(How to cook frozen chicken burgers: https://www.bellandevans.com/product/chicken-burgers/ )
