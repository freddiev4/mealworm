# Sunday

Lunch:
Caprese salad with balsamic glaze (vegetarian, light — good for a Sunday lunch)

Ingredients:
- Ripe tomatoes (2-3)
- Fresh mozzarella (8 oz)
- Fresh basil leaves
- Extra-virgin olive oil
- Balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar + a little honey to reduce
- Salt & pepper

Recipe/link: https://www.onceuponachef.com/recipes/caprese-salad-balsamic-glaze.html

Dinner:
Baja-style fish tacos with cabbage slaw & avocado crema (fish)

Ingredients:
- White fish fillets (cod or tilapia), 1 lb
- Corn or flour tortillas
- Green/red cabbage, shredded
- Lime (2)
- Cilantro
- Avocado (1-2)
- Greek yogurt or sour cream (1/2 cup)
- Garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, pepper
- Red onion or pickled onion (optional)
- Oil for cooking

Sauce: Avocado crema (avocado + yogurt + lime + salt)

Recipe/link: https://rachlmansfield.com/easy-fish-tacos-with-avocado-crema/  (baked/oven version with avocado crema and slaw)
